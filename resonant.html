About Us

Resonant Equipment Private Limited, established in 2023, is at the forefront of high voltage transformer manufacturing in Bikaner, Rajasthan. Our cutting-edge facility is designed to meet the growing demands of India's renewable energy sector.

Our Vision

To become a global leader in Transformer Manufacturing, specializing in transformers up to 220 kV, and to secure a prominent position in the industry within the next five years.

Our Mission

To establish a self-reliant manufacturing facility producing high-quality Distribution/Power Transformers, focusing on meeting local demand, particularly within the Indian renewable market and Railways sector.

Our Products

Power Transformers

High-capacity transformers for power transmission networks.

Distribution Transformers

Efficient transformers for distributing electricity to end-users.

Furnace Transformers

Specialized transformers for industrial furnace applications.

Traction Transformers

Robust transformers designed for railway electrification systems.

Inverter Duty Transformers

Transformers optimized for renewable energy systems.

Compact Substations

Space-efficient substation solutions for urban and industrial areas.
